Introduction

About Me

Pioneering Intelligent Architectures

I am a passionate AI Researcher and Engineer with a background in AI, including work on large language models and medical data processing. I am currently a Research Assistant at the University of Southern Denmark (SDU). My focus is on developing advanced AI solutions for healthcare and other domains.

Over the course of my academic and industry collaborations, I have designed pipelines for neurodegenerative classification (such as Autism Spectrum Disorder and Alzheimer's disease), engineered robust LLM pipelines integrating RAG and agentic workflows, and collaborated on sustainability data sciences. I thrive on developing optimized, explainable, and clean codebases that translate complex mathematical models into real-world applications.

Core Research Fields

ML & Deep Learning

Architecting neural networks (DANNs, GNNs) for advanced pattern recognition.

Generative AI & LLMs

Building end-to-end LLM fine-tuning pipelines, agentic RAG and FastAPI backends.

Medical Image and Signal Processing

Decomposing complex biological series (fMRI, MRI, X-rays) for early disease detection.

Explainable AI (XAI)

Formulating highly interpretable diagnostics that physicians and stakeholders can trust.
